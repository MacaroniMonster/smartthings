smartthings

all my smartthings junk Devices and smartapps I've built for my use, others may use and abuse as they see fit.

aeonSwitch: Variation of the stock SmartThings "Dimmer-Switch" and twack's improved dimmer auto re-configure after setting preferences alarm indicator capability (using AEON hardware blink function) Includes: preferences tile for setting: reporting functions (parameter 80) control switch type (parameter 120) preconfigured blinker modes

aeonDimmer: Variation of the stock SmartThings "Dimmer-Switch" and twack's improved dimmer auto re-configure after setting preferences alarm indicator capability (using AEON hardware blink function) up/down dimmer tiles, with configurable interval rates Includes: preferences tile for setting: reporting functions (parameter 80) control switch type (parameter 120) preconfigured blinker modes
